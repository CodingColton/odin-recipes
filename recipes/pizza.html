Pepperoni Pizza

This recipe is a huge hit with friends and a great alternative to restaurants.

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put the ingredients together in the shape of a pizza.
  2. Put the pizza in the oven at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
  3. Enjoy!
